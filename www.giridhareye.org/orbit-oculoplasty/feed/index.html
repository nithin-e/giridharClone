Educational Qualifications

Dr. Shebin Salim holds an MBBS and a DNB in Ophthalmology. He further specialized in Oculoplasty, completing a Fellowship in Oculoplasty, Orbit, and Ocular Aesthetics at the Sankara Nethralaya Medical Research Foundation.

Specialisation

She specializes in Ophthalmology with a focus on Oculoplasty. Her expertise extends to the management of orbital and ocular conditions, including reconstructive procedures and aesthetic enhancements.

Work Experience

With 4 years of experience as a consultant, Dr. Salim has been actively involved in the field of ophthalmology, providing specialized care and treatment in Oculoplasty. Her work encompasses clinical practice as well as surgical interventions.

Publications

Dr. Shebin Salim has contributed extensively to the field with several publications. Notable works include “Another brick in the wall” in the Indian Journal of Ophthalmology (2018), a case report on superior ophthalmic vein thrombosis post carotid compression (Orbit, 2021), and an article titled “An eye for a tooth: Orbital teratoma in an adult” (Saudi Journal of Ophthalmology, 2022). His other publications cover topics like volumetric analysis in dermis fat graft hypertrophy management, orbital cysticercosis clinical features, and primary leiomyosarcoma of the orbit and conjunctiva. Additionally, a study on orbital and adnexal sarcoidosis has been accepted for publication in the Indian Journal of Ophthalmology.

Achievements

She has received numerous accolades, including second prize for a video presentation at the Mid-Term OPAI 2018 for “Learning curve in endoscopic dacryocystorhinostomy” and first prize at the Mid-Term OPAIC 2019 for a video on “Arterio venous malformations.” She was also honored with the Dr. K C Sankara Menon Award for the best paper at KSOS DRISHTI 2021 and the TACO poster award at the same event.
