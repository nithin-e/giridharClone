A Proud Achievement for 𝗚𝗶𝗿𝗶𝗱𝗵𝗮𝗿 𝗘𝘆𝗲 𝗜𝗻𝘀𝘁𝗶𝘁𝘂𝘁𝗲.

The consultants of Giridhar Eye Institute made a significant presence and academic contribution at the 𝟴𝟰𝘁𝗵 𝗔𝗻𝗻𝘂𝗮𝗹 𝗖𝗼𝗻𝗳𝗲𝗿𝗲𝗻𝗰𝗲 𝗼𝗳 𝘁𝗵𝗲 𝗔𝗹𝗹 𝗜𝗻𝗱𝗶𝗮 𝗢𝗽𝗵𝘁𝗵𝗮𝗹𝗺𝗼𝗹𝗼𝗴𝗶𝗰𝗮𝗹 𝗦𝗼𝗰𝗶𝗲𝘁𝘆, held in Jaipur in March 2026.

We are proud to share that 𝗗𝗿. 𝗠𝗮𝗿𝗶𝗮𝗻 𝗣𝗮𝘂𝗹𝘆, Head of the Department of Oculoplasty and Orbit, received the prestigious 𝗦𝘂𝗷𝗮𝘁𝗮 𝗦𝗮𝘃𝗶𝘁𝗿𝗶 𝗥𝗮𝗼 𝗔𝘄𝗮𝗿𝗱 for her outstanding scientific presentation titled:
“𝙊𝙪𝙩𝙘𝙤𝙢𝙚𝙨 𝙤𝙛 𝙎𝙤𝙘𝙠𝙚𝙩 𝙍𝙚𝙘𝙤𝙣𝙨𝙩𝙧𝙪𝙘𝙩𝙞𝙤𝙣 𝙞𝙣 𝙋𝙖𝙩𝙞𝙚𝙣𝙩𝙨 𝙍𝙚𝙦𝙪𝙞𝙧𝙞𝙣𝙜 𝙈𝙪𝙡𝙩𝙞𝙥𝙡𝙚 𝙎𝙪𝙧𝙜𝙞𝙘𝙖𝙡 𝙄𝙣𝙩𝙚𝙧𝙫𝙚𝙣𝙩𝙞𝙤𝙣𝙨.”
In her presentation, Dr. Marian shared insights from more than a decade of experience in managing complex and challenging socket reconstruction cases.
Her work, reflecting deep clinical expertise and dedication to patient care, received wide appreciation and accolades from peers and experts at the conference.

This recognition highlights the institute’s continued commitment to clinical excellence, research, and innovation in ophthalmology.

Congratulations to Dr. Marian Pauly on this remarkable achievement!

A proud and memorable moment in the history of our institution as our Founder and Director Dr A.Giridhar was conferred the prestigious AIOC S. S. Badrinath Endowment Award during the 84th Annual Conference of the All India Ophthalmological Society, held at the Jaipur Exhibition & Convention Centre on 12 March.

This honour truly belongs to the entire team at Giridhar Eye Institute, recognizing their selfless service and unwavering commitment to eye care over the past three decades. It stands as an appreciation of the remarkable work carried out by our institution and a testament to our dedication to excellence in ophthalmology.

Receiving this recognition at the national level, in the presence of over 10,000 ophthalmologists from across the country, is both humbling and inspiring. It motivates us to continue striving for greater impact and to serve our patients with even greater dedication in the years ahead.
